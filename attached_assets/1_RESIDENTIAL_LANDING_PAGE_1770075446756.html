Frequently Asked Questions

❓ Do you really not check credit?

Absolutely not! No hard inquiries, no soft inquiries, nothing. Bad credit? No credit? Recent bankruptcy? No problem! Everyone is approved.

❓ What does "prepaid" mean?

You pay for service before you use it (like a prepaid phone). When you sign up, you pay for your first month. Then each month, we charge your card 5 days before renewal. No surprise bills or collections calls!

❓ Can I really cancel anytime?

Yes! Just give us 30 days notice by calling (346) 309-5514 or emailing billing@bluemogul.biz. Your service continues through the prepaid period. No early termination fees.

❓ Is there really no data cap?

Truly unlimited! No caps, no throttling, no overage fees. AT&T and Comcast have 1TB caps—we don't. Stream, game, and download as much as you want.

❓ Do I need a cable box for Sling TV?

No! Sling TV streams over your internet. Watch on Smart TV, Roku, Fire TV, Apple TV, phone, tablet, or computer. No equipment rental fees!

❓ How fast is installation?

Most installations within 3-7 business days after payment. Frontier sends a technician to install the fiber. Same-day or next-day may be available—call (346) 309-5514 to ask!

❓ Do you service my address?

We serve most of Houston and surrounding areas where Frontier Fiber is available. Fill out the form below or call (346) 309-5514 to check availability at your address!

❓ What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit cards, debit cards, ACH bank transfer, and cash (in-person). All payments are processed securely through Stripe.
